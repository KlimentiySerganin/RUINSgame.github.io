About RUINS

RUINS - Multiplayer game based on RUST. In this game, it's every man for himself. Don't trust anyone. Survive. Fight. Gather resources. Explore.

Game lore: You are the last survivors of a disaster at a CY-BA reactor connected to a teleport. As a result, you were all teleported to a large island where a civilization once existed. Now, you are forced to fight against similar people.
